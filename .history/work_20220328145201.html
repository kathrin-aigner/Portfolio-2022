Weather psychic
Frontend-developement | UX-design

Ever wondered if a medium can predict the weather for you?
The answer is: A-B-S-O-L-U-T-E-L-Y-! With this app you can ask psychics from all over the world what how the weather will be. Not sure what to bring for your Barcelona trip? Ask weather psychics. Not sure when the best weather for lying in bed and watching movies will be? Ask weather psychic. Shopping tour in a mall on a sunny day? Nah, better ask weather psychic when rain will happen!
